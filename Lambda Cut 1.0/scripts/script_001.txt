Title: Script 1: The Whispers of the Mines

I’m standing here, the air thick with the ghosts of the past, and I can feel their unrest. The captain, a man hardened by war, refuses to act, and that inaction is what’s truly fueling this nightmare. He’s keeping the healthy and the sick walled in, burying them all, and lying about it. *“What else he walled them in minus nurses soldiers the healthy or the sick he buried them all and then he lied about it.”* My own husband died in the dark, a victim of his decisions. This whole mess, this rage in the mines, it all stems from his quarantine. I'm being asked to go into those mines, to find out what’s driving them mad, because the captain won't. *“I would like you to go into the mines and find out what in rage is them so I would like you to do what the captain will not.”* They say the truth lies down there, but what if the truth is even more terrifying than the specters themselves? And as I prepare to descend, I can’t shake the feeling that the captain has far more sinister plans, like locking me away as he did that innocent woman in New Eden. *“The captain has a penchant for locking people up and leaving them to rot.”* But as I head towards the hidden tunnel, a chilling whisper reaches my ears, not from the mines, but from the fort itself, warning me that the captain isn’t the only one playing dangerous games.
