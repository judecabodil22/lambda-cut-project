Title: Script 2: The Weight of Guilt

I’m standing here, surrounded by darkness, trying to understand a man haunted by his past. He’s trapped in a cycle of guilt, unable to move forward. He speaks of a mission gone wrong, a friend he failed to save. *"I was trying to save him and I shot him."* It’s a wound that festers, preventing him from living. *"As long as he swallows his guilt, nothing will change."* He blames himself for the death of his friend, Andrew White, and that blame is a heavy burden. *"I've carried the weight of your death as far as I can."* Now, I have to help him confront this specter, to find a way for him to finally let go. But what if the true cost of his past is something I can't comprehend, something that will cost us all?
